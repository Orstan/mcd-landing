1. Overview

Welcome to the McD Token documentation. Here you will find instructions on how to connect your wallet, purchase tokens, view tokenomics, and interact with key features of our platform.

2. Connecting Your Wallet

  1. Click the Connect Wallet button in the site's header.
  2. Select your wallet provider (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect).
  3. Authorize the connection in your wallet extension or mobile app.
  4. Once connected, your address will appear on the button, and you're ready to proceed.

3. Purchasing McD Tokens

  1. Ensure your wallet is connected and that you have sufficient MATIC (or other supported tokens).
  2. Enter the amount of MATIC you wish to spend in the Buy section.
  3. Review the token amount you will receive based on the current rate (e.g., 1000 McD per 1 MATIC).
  4. Click Confirm Purchase and approve the transaction in your wallet.
  5. After confirmation, your McD Token balance will update automatically.
